Company HR Policies

Leave Policy:
- Employees get 18 paid leaves per year.
- Unused leaves can be carried forward to next year up to 10 days.

Working Hours:
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Probation:
- New employees are on probation for 3 months.

Benefits:
- Health insurance is provided after 1 month of joining.
- Employees are eligible for a performance bonus once a year.
